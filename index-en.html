Who am I?

Hi, my name is Rafael A. Milanés. Welcome to my CV/Portfolio. I am a newly graduated software developer whose mission is to find the best possible job or internship, below I welcome you to find out who I am and why there would be no chance of choosing wrongfully when trusting responsibilities with me in your company :).

I studied Software development in Java at the Newton Institute of Malmö, Sweden. I have always wanted to be a programmer but never actually dared taking the first hard steps of just getting my hands dirty with problem-solving. Right after I started with my first JavaScript tutorial in May 2019 my every-day life has changed radically, I have cultivated a need for learning new things related to programming every single day and as gratitude to all the teachers at my school and tutorial platforms like Udemy and YouTube I have started passing on the knowledge I’ve acquired to the new generation of coders, I teach an online class of 12 year-olds in Spain. It is said that the best way to learn anything is by transmitting it to others.

After almost two years with only focus on programming and problem solving, I am absolutely read to use my acquired skills in a professional team, working on projects that actually matter. I have not only focused on Java, and this will be reflected in the rest of my humble curriculum awaiting below. My efforts have also been dedicated to web technologies like JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5 and the well-known ReactJS library which has been of great help in my first small projects. I am currently studying the MERN-stack on a deeper level to be completely prepared to work as Full-Stack Developer
